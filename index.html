Mission

We are building a protein design company for the next era of human health, where computational models, structural insight, and translational biology work together to address diseases that emerge from cellular decline, uncontrolled growth, and loss of organ function.

Science

Protein structure is a language of biological function.

Many complex diseases are driven by changes in signaling, repair, immune balance, and tissue resilience. AlphaGene Labs studies these systems through the shape and behavior of proteins, then uses computational biology to design candidates with specific functional intent.

Designed sequences are refined against target hypotheses, tissue context, and desired biological roles.
